The sacraments

Christ continues his saving work through the sacred mysteries of the Church. We prepare each soul with reverence and joy.

Baptism & Chrismation

For infants, parents and godparents attend parish preparation; baptisms are scheduled with the vicar. In the Syro-Malabar tradition, Chrismation (Confirmation) is ordinarily conferred together with Baptism and first Holy Qurbana for infants.

Adults and older children: journey through Christian initiation.

First Holy Qurbana

Children complete catechism and retreat before first solemn communion. Registration through the Sunday school office.

Confirmation (later reception)

Those baptised as infants in other circumstances may receive Chrismation later—pastoral guidance from the vicar.

Holy Matrimony

Engaged couples should meet the vicar at least six months before the wedding. Marriage preparation includes parish interviews, a pre-marriage course recognised by the Archdiocese of Thrissur, and planning the liturgy (Qurbana or Latin-rite Mass as applicable).

Sacrament of the Sick

Before surgery, in serious illness, or for the elderly—call the parish office. In hospital emergencies, notify the chaplaincy desk.

Funerals

We accompany families in mourning. Coordinate with the parish and funeral directors for the house prayers, funeral Qurbana or Mass, and cemetery rites.
