Our story

Founded by immigrant families in 1912, our parish has served this neighborhood through wars, renewal, and every ordinary day of grace.

Who we are

St. Mary of the Angels is a Roman Catholic parish in the Diocese of Example. We exist to worship God, form disciples, and serve the poor in the name of Jesus Christ.

Our patroness, Mary, Mother of the Church, teaches us to say “yes” to God with humility and courage. We strive to be a home where strangers become friends and friends become saints.

Mission

We celebrate the sacraments faithfully, preach the Gospel plainly, and welcome every person God sends through our doors—whether for an hour or a lifetime.

Belonging

If you are new to the Catholic Church, returning after time away, or simply looking for a parish, we would be honored to walk with you. Start with Mass, introduce yourself to a priest or deacon, or contact the office for registration.
