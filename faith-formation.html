RCIA — Becoming Catholic

The Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults is for those who wish to be baptized, or baptized Christians who seek full communion with the Catholic Church. Weekly sessions run from September through Easter.

  • Inquiry: no commitment—just questions.
  • Catechumenate: deeper formation in the faith.
  • Sacraments of initiation at the Easter Vigil.
