Christian initiation (OCIA)

For adults seeking baptism, or baptized Christians preparing for full communion in the Catholic Church. Inquiry begins after consultation with the vicar; catechesis follows the rhythms of the liturgical year toward Easter.

  • Inquiry: ask questions without pressure.
  • Catechumenate: Scripture, liturgy, and parish life.
  • Sacraments of initiation at the Easter Vigil.
