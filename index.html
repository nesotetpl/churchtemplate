Our parish in Palapuram

St. George Church stands with the faithful of Palapuram as a home of prayer, formation, and service. We celebrate the Holy Qurbana in the Syro-Malabar tradition, walk with families through every season of life, and open our doors to pilgrims and neighbours alike.

Holy Qurbana & Mass

We gather for the Divine Liturgy in the Syro-Malabar rite, with Latin-rite Masses where noted. Times below are sample schedules—confirm in the weekly bulletin or call the office.

Weekend

  • Saturday 5:30 p.m. Holy Qurbana (Malayalam)
  • Sunday 6:30 a.m. Qurbana
  • Sunday 9:00 a.m. with choir
  • Sunday 5:00 p.m. English Mass (Latin rite)

Weekday

  • Mon–Sat 6:15 a.m. Morning Qurbana
  • First Friday 5:00 p.m. Adoration

Confession & feasts

Saturday 4:30–5:15 p.m. and by appointment. Perunnal and holy day times are announced in the bulletin and at parish events.

Sacraments

Christ meets us in the sacraments of the Church. See our sacraments page for baptism, Chrismation, marriage, and anointing.

  • ☩

    Baptism & Chrismation

    Infants and adults: preparation with parents, godparents, and catechists. Adults may journey through Christian initiation.

  • ☩

    Reconciliation

    Before Vigil Qurbana and by appointment. The Father’s mercy is always within reach.

  • ☩

    Holy Matrimony

    Contact the parish at least six months before your wedding. See marriage preparation.

  • ☩

    Sacrament of the Sick

    For serious illness or surgery, call the parish office—day or night—for a priest.
